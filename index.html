03 OPERATING PRINCIPLES
P—1

Trust is the product.

In enterprise AI, accuracy, citations, and compliance aren't polish — they're the adoption curve. Nobody scaled a tool they didn't believe. 60% → 85% accuracy is what took Passport AI from pilot to 10,000 users.

P—2

Telemetry beats opinion.

Production usage decides the roadmap, not the loudest voice in the room. I build the feedback loops first, then let what's actually breaking — and actually used — make the calls. Three major releases in twelve months came out of that loop.

P—3

Escalate early. Ship on time.

Passport AI carried 15+ cross-team dependencies across data, compliance, and infrastructure. I tracked the risks weekly and took three critical blockers to VP level — the $10M feature landed on schedule.

P—4

Meet users in their tools.

Distribution beats destination. The best interface for your product might be someone else's — which is why Euromonitor's data now answers questions inside Copilot, Claude, and customers' own agents.
